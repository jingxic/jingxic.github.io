IV.Lydian

"The Haunting Scale"


Intervals: 2-2-2-1-2-2-1

It begins with three whole tones, then a semitone, followed by two whole tones and finally, ending with a semitone. Using Lydian will help you craft chord progressions and melodies with a distinct sound that is arresting, haunting and effective.

You can feel the sound of this music mode by playing with the keyboard on the left.
