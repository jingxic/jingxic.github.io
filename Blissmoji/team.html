Team

We are an team of passionate product developers based at Carnegie Mellon University. Our members come from various fields: Data Science, Business, Engineering, HCI. With such interdisciplinary background, we wish to close the technology/communication gap for many, and hopefully facilitate the use of Emoji by people of all backgrounds, ages, and levels of ability.
